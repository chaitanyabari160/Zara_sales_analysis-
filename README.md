# Zara_sales_analysis-
Title - Zara sales analysis dashboard

Purpose - 
To track and analyze Zara's sales performance, identify top-selling products and categories, evaluate promotion effectiveness, and support data-driven business decisions.

Tech stack -
* Power BI – Dashboard design, visualization, and reporting.
* Excel / CSV Dataset – Data source for sales records.
* Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation.
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Calculated measures, KPIs, and analytics.
* Data Modeling – Relationships and business logic for reporting.

Highlight -

*Business problem -
Zara needs to understand sales performance across different product categories, customer segments, store placements, and promotions. Without clear insights, it is difficult to identify top-selling products, optimize inventory, evaluate marketing campaigns, and make informed decisions to maximize revenue and profitability.

* Goal of dashboard - 
To provide a comprehensive view of Zara's sales performance by tracking key metrics, identifying sales trends, analyzing product and customer behavior, and enabling data-driven decisions to improve revenue, inventory management, and marketing effectiveness.

* Walk through key values - 
1. KPI Cards (Top Section)
Displays key metrics: Sales Volume (456K), Total Sales (9.52bn), and Product Count (250) for a quick performance overview.
2. Sales by Product Category (Donut Chart)
Shows the contribution of each category to total sales.
Jackets lead with 55.2% of total sales.
3. Sales by Gender (Pie Chart)
Compares sales between Men and Women products.
Women's products contribute slightly more sales than men's.
4. Sales by Product Position (Bar Chart)
Analyzes sales based on store placement (Aisle, End-cap, Front of Store).
Helps evaluate the effectiveness of product positioning.
5. Sales by Promotion (Bar Chart)
Compares sales generated from promoted and non-promoted products.
Measures the impact of promotional campaigns on revenue.
6. Top 5 Products by Sales (Bar Chart)
Highlights the highest-performing products.
Helps identify best-sellers and inventory priorities.
7. Filters/Slicers
Allows users to filter data by Product Category, Seasonal Status, and Promotion Status for detailed analysis.
